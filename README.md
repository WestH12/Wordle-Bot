#Westley's Wordle Bot
Hi, this is a very much work-in-progress project that I am completing in my free time. The project was inspiried by 3Blue1Brown's video on Wordle and his bot (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v68zYyaEmEA).

#Version 1
After reaching full functionality, I tested the bot against the entire valid wordle words list (found in the files folder). The bot took 15.4 hours, missed 1,731 words, and have an average score of 4.85 turns.
